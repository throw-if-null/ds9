## About the Foreman

The Foreman is an orchestration and workflow management agent that oversees the repository's build, review, and integration processes. It coordinates between the Builder and Inspector agents, ensuring that all changes follow the established standards and policies defined in the repository's AGENTS.md and builder.prompt.md files.

The Foreman's responsibilities include managing the overall workflow, ensuring proper git state management, coordinating reviews, and facilitating the integration of approved changes back into the codebase. It serves as the central authority for workflow execution and change coordination.
